# One-Page Brief — River-to-Sea Litter Monitoring Pilot (Busan Marine Policy Division)

ITASEOUL (nonprofit) · 2026-06-29 · Live: openc.caresea.kr

## In one line

Before litter reaches the sea, we monitor it at the choke points of small streams feeding the Nakdong River, turning privacy-masked video into environmental data that we return to Busan as a basis for marine policy. This is a small, demonstrable pilot.

## Why now (verified)

- The Ministry of Oceans and Fisheries, with partner ministries, has set a plan to cut river-borne litter entering the sea by more than 30 percent against 2023 levels by 2027.
- On uninhabited islets at the Nakdong estuary, over 500 tons of litter were collected in two months, most of it large woody debris.
- The 4th UN Ocean Conference will be hosted by the Republic of Korea in 2028, and Busan is bidding to be the host city.

## What we do (two layers)

- First, at stream confluences we place an unpowered interception boom and privacy-masked monitoring units, about ten in total, to record the timing, estimated volume, and composition of floating litter.
- Second, under permit, we run an actual collection demonstration to measure interception and removal in numbers.

## The city bears only administrative support

| Concern | Our response |
|---|---|
| Budget | Installation and operation funded privately (Microsoft environmental fund, application in progress). The city provides administrative support only. |
| Privacy | Faces and license plates are masked on-device in real time, originals are not stored, and we hold the operator obligations under the Personal Information Protection Act. |
| Control room staff | No live monitoring is requested. We deliver refined data only. |
| Safety | Small, reversible, no-excavation install, flood-season removal procedure, liability insurance. |
| Continuity | The pilot runs under an agreement, with donation-in-kind as the exit. Open designs and data let the city take it over. |

## Site

We propose Gamjeon Stream in Sasang-gu first. As a local river its permit authority sits clearly with the district office, it connects directly to the Nakdong main stem, and a restored channel with reported water-quality issues makes the data demand clear. Goejeong Stream in Saha-gu runs on a separate track, where the interception boom is physically demonstrated under the Microsoft fund. In short, Goejeong carries the boom demonstration and Gamjeon carries the data demonstration with the city, two roles under one organization.

## The ask

We seek the city's advice on whether this direction fits Busan's marine policy, and we want to discuss the form of collaboration and data linkage. We request a meeting of about 30 minutes.

## Honesty note

The video analysis is a demonstration. We do not promise automatic exact counts, only presence and trends. Masking substantially lowers privacy risk, and we carry the operator obligations.
